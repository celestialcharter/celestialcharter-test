The Vessel

Built for Speed,
Made for Freedom

The Corto is a compact yet powerful open speedboat, perfect for intimate charters along Montenegro's spectacular coastline. With capacity for up to 6 guests, she offers a more personal and exhilarating on-water experience.

Her agile handling makes her perfect for exploring secluded bays, snorkelling spots, and coastal villages that larger vessels cannot reach. Fast, fun, and effortlessly stylish.
