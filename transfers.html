Why Go by Boat

The Road Goes Around.
We Go Across.

The Bay of Kotor is shaped like a folded map. By land, every destination requires a long drive around the water's edge — through traffic, narrow mountain roads, and summer gridlock. By sea, you arrive in minutes, and the journey itself is the experience.

3×

Faster on Average

A boat transfer from Tivat to Herceg Novi takes 30 minutes. By road in summer? Over 90.

Traffic, Ever

No roundabouts, no summer queues, no waiting at the Kamenari ferry. The sea is always open.
