Who We Are

Five Years Mastering
Boka Bay

Founded by passionate sailors with deep roots in Montenegro, Celestial Charter was built on one belief: every journey on the sea should feel extraordinary. What started as a single vessel and a shared passion has grown into Tivat's premier luxury charter company.

Over five years, we have carefully built a fleet of own and partner vessels, assembled an experienced crew with unmatched local knowledge, and hosted hundreds of guests who return season after season.

  • Based in Tivat — home of Porto Montenegro
  • Own fleet plus handpicked partner vessels
  • Licensed, certified crew with deep local expertise
  • Available 7 days a week, 9am – 9pm
